Hi! My name is Galina, and I proudly wear the hat of an engineer. It's not just my profession, but also the lens through which I perceive the world around me.
With over 10 years under my belt as a software engineer, Python has been my steadfast companion, guiding me through the intricacies of the tech world. My journey in software engineering began with the versatile C/C++, which opened a world of possibilities and granted me the freedom to explore various facets of technology. This exploration led me to delve deep into information security and frontend development, areas that I find incredibly fascinating.
Over time, I have honed my skills in React and React Native, finding as much joy and satisfaction in them as I do in Python and C. The beauty of these technologies is their ability to bring ideas to life, to shape them into tangible solutions that have the potential to make a real difference.
In the past, I had the opportunity to immerse myself in the field of research, specifically in the area of domain-specific languages and compilers. My focus was on MLIR progressive lowering and advanced optimizations for parallel and emerging computing systems. This period of my career was incredibly rewarding as it allowed me to contribute to the ever-evolving world of technology and pave the way for future innovations.
Currently, I’m working as a Software Engineer at nachtblau.tv, where I contribute to innovative media and broadcasting solutions. I specialize in backend development using Python, OpenSearch, Redis, TorchServe, and containerized environments with Docker Swarm. My work focuses on building scalable data pipelines, deploying ML inference services, and ensuring observability with tools like Prometheus and Grafana—all within a fast-paced, production-oriented environment.
I am always open to new challenges and opportunities. If you have something that might pique my interest, please feel free to get in touch with me. I'm always excited to explore new horizons and expand my professional network.
